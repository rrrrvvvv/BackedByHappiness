Spend rewards in two ways

Loyalty Rewards or Profit Sharing

Tokens spent each way are subject to different rules and conditions

Loyalty Rewards

  • Users earn tokens with each dollar spent
  • Users redeem tokens for discounts or free items at participating ethical establishments
  • Token cost of each item is set by the establishment
  • Default mode of redemption

Profit Sharing

  • Users can opt to use their earned tokens to claim a share of profits earned by Backed By Happiness to spend at participating locations
  • Users earn tokens with each dollar spent, however the rate at which they earn tokens decreases over time
  • This creates an incentive for users to join the netowrk early
  • A percentage of profits earned by the network are pooled and used to buy back tokens that users redeem
  • Users can buy and sell tokens to other users via the token trading exchange
