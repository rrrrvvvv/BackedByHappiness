What is Backed By Happiness?

Backed By Happiness is a loyalty network, similar to airmiles, that allows you to earn rewards by shopping at only the most ethical businesses in their class. A class of business is defined by the service they provide, breweries are one class of business, coffee shops are another.

How does it work?

Backed By Happiness works to ensure a high ethical standard for the businesses that are part of the loyalty network by auditing their operations periodically. The results of the audit are scored and the participating businesses are ranked. Only the highest ranking businesses are selected to be a part of the network.

Learn more about our ranking criteria here

How do I get involved?

Joining the network is as easy as creating an account and using the web app on your mobile device when visiting any of the eligible businesses. Once you are signed up, click on the collect tab to see where you can start earning rewards.
